# Sales: Draft Outreach

Craft personalized outreach emails across three primary contexts in industrial real estate: LP prospecting, tenant leasing, and broker relationships.

## Key Principles
- Lead with value or insight, not a pitch
- Keep cold outreach under 150 words
- Research-backed personalization
- Specific hook tied to their situation
- One clear call-to-action

## Three Core Templates

**LP Prospect**
Focus on fund performance metrics and alignment with investor mandates. Request a brief exploratory call.

**Tenant Outreach**
Lead with available space specifications and submarket relevance. Offer direct engagement before broader marketing.

**Broker Relationship**
Highlight property availability and co-broke terms. Position as preferred first-look partner.

## Pre-Draft Checklist
- Recent company news
- Mutual connections
- Investment focus / space requirements
- Relevant local deal history

## Follow-Up Cadence
| Day | Approach |
|-----|----------|
| Day 1 | Initial outreach |
| Day 5 | Brief follow-up |
| Day 14 | Value-add touchpoint (market insight) |
| Day 30+ | Market insight, no direct ask |

**Key principle:** Specificity beats generic. Personalized research is the foundation for every message.
