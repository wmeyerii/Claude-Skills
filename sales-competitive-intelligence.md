# Sales: Competitive Intelligence

Structured framework for analyzing competitors in industrial real estate markets.

## Four-Step Methodology

**Step 1 — Property Comparison Table**
Compare across: property size, clear height, dock doors, rental rates, and occupancy.

**Step 2 — Market Positioning**
Identify tenant alternatives, understand decision drivers, and map competitive advantages and disadvantages.

**Step 3 — Competing Fund Research**
Analyze: AUM, strategy focus, recent deal activity, and LP positioning.

**Step 4 — Competitive Intelligence Brief**
- Executive summary
- Comp analysis table
- Market conditions
- Positioning assessment
- Strategic implications

## Primary Research Sources
CoStar, CREXI, LoopNet, commercial brokerage reports, broker conversations, and SEC filings.

## Output
Completed competitive intelligence brief with:
- Comparison tables
- Market summary
- Positioning analysis
- Actionable strategic recommendations for leasing, pricing, or fund positioning
