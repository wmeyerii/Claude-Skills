# Industrial Real Estate Acquisition Memo Template

This document outlines a standardized framework for building investment-ready acquisition memos for industrial real estate deals.

## Key Purpose
The template is designed for Investment Committee approval, LP co-investor updates, deal documentation, and pre-LOI evaluation.

## Core Structure
The memo follows a seven-section format:

1. **Executive Summary** — One-page overview with deal metrics (property, market, square footage, price, cap rate), strategy classification, target returns (IRR, cash-on-cash, equity multiple), and a clear recommendation

2. **Property Overview** — Physical characteristics, condition assessment, and functional limitations

3. **Market Analysis** — Vacancy, absorption, rent trends, and comparable transaction/lease data

4. **Tenancy & Lease Analysis** — Tenant roster with credit quality and weighted average lease expiration (WALE)

5. **Financial Underwriting** — Sources & uses, pro forma NOI, return calculations, and sensitivity analyses

6. **Value Creation Plan** — Specific initiatives with timelines and risk-adjusted upside

7. **Risk Factors** — Deal-specific risks paired with mitigation strategies

## Quality Standards
Returns must be calculated, not estimated. Market sources must be cited. Risks must be specific to the deal. Recommendations must be unambiguous.
