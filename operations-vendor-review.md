# Operations: Vendor Review

Evaluate vendors and contractors for industrial real estate through a structured five-step process.

## Weighted Scoring Matrix
| Criteria | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| Price competitiveness | 25% |
| Relevant experience | 20% |
| Licensing & insurance | 20% |
| References/reputation | 15% |
| Capacity/availability | 10% |
| Communication quality | 10% |

## Contract Review Focus
Flag problematic terms: no cap on liability, auto-renewal clauses without notice window, missing indemnification language.

## Reference Check Questions (5 standard)
1. Did they complete on time?
2. Were they on budget?
3. How was their responsiveness?
4. Were there quality issues?
5. Would you rehire them?

## Red Flags — Do Not Engage If:
- Absent or expired insurance
- Lack of required licensing
- No verifiable business address
- Unresolved negative reviews
- Refusal to provide references

## Deliverables
- Completed scoring matrix
- Contract summary
- Reference notes
- Recommendation memo (selected vendor, rationale, contract value, identified risks, next steps)
