# Deep Research Protocol

Conduct comprehensive research on industrial real estate topics including markets, tenants, competitors, and regulatory matters.

## Three-Step Framework

**Step 1 — Define the Research Question**
Specify geography, timeframe, and focus area before beginning.

**Step 2 — Source Selection**
| Research Type | Primary Sources |
|---------------|----------------|
| Market data | CBRE/JLL/Cushman reports, CoStar, port/postal data |
| Tenant/company | SEC EDGAR, IBISWorld, Fed Beige Book, BLS employment |
| Regulatory | Local planning depts, state environmental agencies, EPA ECHO, permit records |
| Fund/competitor | SEC EDGAR, fund databases, broker conversations |

**Step 3 — Research Structure**
- Executive summary
- Background
- Themed sections with data tables
- Risk assessment
- Conclusions with confidence ratings

## Core Standards
- Cite sources for every material fact
- Date your data — market data ages quickly in RE
- Distinguish interpretation from factual findings
- Quantify uncertainty ranges (not single estimates)
- Flag conflicting data explicitly

## Output: Standardized Report
1. Executive Summary (key findings, confidence level, gaps)
2. Background
3. Analysis sections (market / company / regulatory / competitive)
4. Data tables
5. Risk assessment
6. Conclusions and recommended next steps
