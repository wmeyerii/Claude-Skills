# Finance: Audit Support

Procedures for preparing audit support materials for industrial real estate entities.

## Primary Functions
Assist with preparing PBC (Prepared by Client) lists and audit schedules for LP audits, lender audits, and external financial statement audits.

## Standard PBC Categories
Financial statements, bank records, debt agreements, equity records, revenue documentation, expenses, fixed assets, accruals, intercompany transactions, and legal documents.

## Core Deliverables
1. A PBC tracker monitoring requests, assignees, due dates, and completion status
2. Audit support schedules showing GL balances, reconciling items, and supporting documentation
3. Prepared responses to common auditor inquiries regarding revenue, liabilities, and related-party transactions

## Process Requirements
Communications with auditors must include acknowledgment within 24 hours, status updates on outstanding items, flags for items requiring additional time, and controller review before sharing draft documents.

## Typical Support Areas
Rent recognition per lease terms, CAM reconciliations, accrual methodologies based on invoices and contracts, and disclosure of affiliate transactions and management fees.
