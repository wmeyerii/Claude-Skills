# Industrial Lease Abstraction Tool

This resource provides a structured framework for extracting and summarizing critical terms from industrial lease agreements. It's designed to streamline lease review processes across multiple use cases—from initial due diligence to portfolio audits and tenant risk assessment.

## Key Components

The tool organizes lease data into six primary categories:

**Parties & Property Details** capture landlord/tenant identities, property location, square footage, and asset classification.

**Term & Dates** section documents commencement, expiration, renewal options with notice requirements, and any early termination provisions.

**Rent Structure** breaks down base rates, escalation schedules, abatement periods, and expense responsibility models (NNN, gross, or modified gross).

**Operating Expenses** identify CAM charges, tax/insurance/utility allocations, and relevant caps or audit provisions.

**Use & Operations** defines permitted activities, hazardous materials handling, and operational constraints.

**TI & Assignment Rights** address tenant improvement allowances, alteration permissions, and assignment/subletting consent requirements.

## Red Flags Noted
Co-tenancy clauses, below-market rents with short remaining terms, weak personal guarantees, unrestricted assignment rights, hazardous materials gaps, and expiring CAM caps.

The output format presents findings as a structured abstract with a rent schedule table and flagged issues for rapid decision-making.
