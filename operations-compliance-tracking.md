# Operations: Compliance Tracking

Monitor regulatory, lease, and reporting obligations in industrial real estate management.

## Master Compliance Register
Track obligations with: ID numbers, type (Insurance, Lease, REIT, etc.), property, frequency, due date, responsible owner, and status.

## Status Codes
- 🟢 Green — Completed
- 🟡 Yellow — Active/in progress
- 🟠 Orange — Due within 14 days
- 🔴 Red — Overdue
- ⬜ White — Outside 30-day window

## Primary Compliance Categories
- **Lease-related:** repairs, CAM reconciliation, estoppel certificates
- **Regulatory:** environmental monitoring, fire inspections, permits
- **REIT obligations:** asset/income tests, distribution requirements

## Alert Protocol
| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 30 days out | Assign responsibility, verify requirements |
| 14 days out | Check progress, flag delays |
| 7 days out | Confirm completion timelines |
| Overdue | Escalate to management, assess penalty exposure |

**Frequency:** Weekly review | **Output:** 90-day deadline report with recommended actions
