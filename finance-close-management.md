# Finance: Close Management

Structured approach for managing month-end and quarter-end closes in industrial real estate funds. Designed for controllers coordinating financial close activities across multiple entities.

## Four-Stage Framework

**Pre-Close Phase**: Verify invoices, cash receipts, and bank statement availability before close begins.

**Transaction Close (Days 1–3)**: Journal entries for depreciation, accruals, intercompany clearing, and bank reconciliations.

**Review & Reporting (Days 4–7)**: Controller review, variance analysis, financial statement drafting, and management sign-off.

**Quarter-End Additions**: Debt compliance checks, capital account reconciliation, preferred return calculations, and LP reporting.

## Escalation Protocol
Flag close delays exceeding two business days, unexplained variances over $10,000, or missing property manager data as items requiring management attention.

## Output
Task ownership templates with due dates and status columns. Status format uses checkmarks and progress indicators for management communication.

**Frequency:** Monthly | **Owner:** Controller
