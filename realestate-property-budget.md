# Industrial Property Annual Budget Tool

This resource guides budgeting for industrial real estate assets. It's structured around three primary components:

**Revenue Analysis**: Build income tenant by tenant and account for vacancy using market-justified assumptions, plus ancillary revenue streams like parking or antenna fees.

**Operating Expenses**: Covers standard categories (taxes, insurance, utilities, maintenance, management fees) organized in a comparison format tracking budget year against prior actuals with variance notation.

**Capital Planning**: Projects are categorized by type—lifecycle items (roof, HVAC), lease-driven tenant improvements, and discretionary value-add initiatives—with cost, timing, and funding source tracked.

The resource emphasizes variance analysis for fluctuations exceeding 5% or $5,000, distinguishing between recurring and one-time drivers. It also requires a detailed assumptions page documenting occupancy, rent growth, expense escalation, and reserve methodologies.

**Typical use cases** include Q3–Q4 annual budget prep, mid-year reforecasting, and asset management reviews with ownership stakeholders.
