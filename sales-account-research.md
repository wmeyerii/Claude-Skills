# Sales: Account Research

Research prospective tenants, investors, or partners for industrial real estate — evaluating company profile, financials, industry position, and creditworthiness.

## Primary Use Cases
- Tenant credit assessment before lease execution
- LP prospect evaluation ahead of investor meetings
- Joint venture partner vetting
- Co-investor background checks
- Advisor firm assessments

## Research Approaches by Category

**Tenant Creditworthiness Assessment**
Gather via D&B reports, public financial filings, news alerts for distress signals, LinkedIn growth metrics, and Glassdoor culture indicators. Street-level facility verification adds operational insight.
Output: company profile, industrial footprint, identified risk factors, and proceed/decline recommendation.

**Investor Fit Analysis**
Review firm websites, SEC filings for registered advisors, team backgrounds, and fund databases. Assess AUM, decision-makers, asset allocation targets, check sizes, and return expectations.

**JV Partner Vetting**
Examine industrial track record, financial capacity, operational strengths, market reputation, and fee structures. Reference checks with existing partners strengthen due diligence.

## Deliverable Format
Structured profile with clear fit assessment and recommended next steps tailored to the prospect type.
