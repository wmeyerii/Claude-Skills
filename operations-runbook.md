# Operations: Runbook / Playbook

Create operational runbooks and playbooks for recurring processes, incidents, and maintenance in industrial property management.

## Key Components Per Runbook
- **Immediate action steps** for the first 15 minutes of an incident
- **Escalation contacts** organized by role and availability
- **Conditional decision trees** to handle variations in situations
- **Documentation requirements** for compliance and post-incident review

## Primary Scenarios Covered
- Emergency situations (HVAC failures, roof leaks, water intrusion)
- Tenant lifecycle procedures (move-in/move-out processes)
- After-hours incident response protocols
- Preventive maintenance scheduling

## Runbook Structure
Each runbook includes:
- Owner and priority level
- Trigger / when to use
- Immediate actions (first 15 min)
- Step-by-step resolution procedure
- Decision tree for variations
- Escalation contacts (by role)
- Resolution criteria
- Documentation required (photos, work orders, notification logs)
- Post-incident review checklist
