# Operations: Risk Assessment Framework

Systematically identify and prioritize operational threats for industrial real estate assets and funds.

## Eight Risk Categories
Physical/property, environmental, leasing/occupancy, financial, market, regulatory, operational, and natural hazard.

## Scoring Methodology
5×5 matrix multiplying Likelihood (1–5) × Impact (1–5):
- **1–4:** Low
- **5–9:** Medium
- **10–16:** High
- **17–25:** Critical

## Four-Step Process
1. Document identified risks with triggers
2. Score using the likelihood-impact matrix
3. Populate a risk register tracking ownership and review dates
4. Develop mitigation strategies: avoid, mitigate, transfer, or accept

## Output Deliverables
- Prioritized risk register
- Heat map summary
- Mitigation action plan with accountability assignments
- Executive summary

## Applications
- Quarterly asset reviews
- Acquisition due diligence
- Capital expenditure decisions
- Portfolio risk reporting
- Insurance renewal support
- Environmental/regulatory compliance assessment
