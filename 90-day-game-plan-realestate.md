# 90-Day Game Plan — Industrial Real Estate & Fund Management

Build a phased action plan for real estate and fund management objectives over 90 days.

## Goal Categories
Choose one: **Leasing** | **Acquisition/Disposition** | **Capital** | **Operations/Reporting**

## Three-Phase Framework

**Phase 1 (Days 1–30) — Foundation**
Decisions, systems, and relationship activation needed to support your goal.
- Actions must be specific enough to calendar
- Identify external players (brokers, lenders, tenants, legal) and map activation steps
- Focus: set up the conditions for success

**Phase 2 (Days 31–60) — Execution**
Move deals toward contract stage, get tenants engaged, secure capital commitments, or launch systems.
- Each action tied to a measurable outcome
- Weekly check-in on progress vs. plan

**Phase 3 (Days 61–90) — Closing**
Finalize signatures, report results, or go live.
- Metrics: signed or unsigned, live or offline

## Requirements for Each Phase
- 3–5 concrete actions
- Measurable outcomes (not vague targets)
- External dependencies identified
- Realistic weekly time commitment (max 15 hours for single operator)

## Getting Started
1. State your specific 90-day goal
2. Identify the biggest external dependency that must be activated immediately
3. Map Phase 1 actions to activate that dependency
