# Finance: SOX Internal Control Testing

Design and execute SOX compliance tests for financial reporting processes in industrial real estate operations.

## Core Testing Methodology
1. Understand controls through walkthroughs
2. Select risk-based samples (typically 25–40 items for monthly processes)
3. Execute testing procedures
4. Document findings using standardized workpapers

## Control Areas
- Revenue/rent collection
- Accounts payable
- Payroll
- Financial close procedures
- Fixed assets management
- Treasury operations
- Consolidated financial reporting

## Documentation Requirements
- Risk & Control Matrix
- Individual control test workpapers for each control examined
- Deficiency summaries
- Remediation plans for identified control gaps

## Deficiency Levels
- **Control Deficiency** — minor gap
- **Significant Deficiency** — could lead to material misstatement
- **Material Weakness** — reasonable possibility of material misstatement in financial statements

## Applications
Annual SOX 404 compliance, audit committee reporting, and internal control documentation for real estate fund operations.
