# Sales: Call Preparation Framework

Prepare for client-facing meetings in industrial real estate.

## Four-Step Framework

1. **Counterparty Research** (5 min) — Review prior interactions, recent news, outstanding items, and specific interests

2. **Position & Messages** — Document your stance on key topics with supporting data points

3. **Q&A Preparation** — Anticipate difficult questions across LP meetings, tenant renewals, and lender discussions with prepared responses

4. **Call Agenda** — Purpose, timing, attendees, key messages, questions to ask, desired outcomes

## Primary Use Cases
LP updates, prospect meetings, lease negotiations, broker calls, covenant discussions, co-investment conversations.

## Output: Call Prep Sheet
- Counterparty background (company, role, recent news)
- Prior relationship history
- Your position on key topics + data points
- Anticipated tough questions + prepared answers
- Meeting agenda
- Walk-away position
- Desired outcome / success criteria
- Open items to resolve

**Key principle:** Know your walk-away position and specific desired outcomes before any conversation begins.
