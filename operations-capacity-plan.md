# Operations: Capacity Planning Framework

Manage capacity across industrial real estate portfolios across four key dimensions.

## Four Dimensions

**Space/Leasing Capacity**
Track available square footage, occupancy rates, and leasing velocity (SF leased per quarter, trailing 4Q average) to forecast supply constraints.

**Team Capacity**
Monitor workload distribution across roles, flagging bottlenecks when staff exceed 80% utilization thresholds.

**CapEx Pipeline Capacity**
Schedule capital projects while accounting for vendor availability, team bandwidth, and fund commitments to prevent resource conflicts.

**Acquisition Pipeline Capacity**
Evaluate organizational readiness before adding assets — assess underwriting bandwidth, property management capacity, and finance resources.

## Output
A capacity snapshot dashboard highlighting:
- Available space
- Team status
- Capital availability
- Top constraints with recommended actions

**Frequency:** Monthly | **Users:** Asset Managers and Operations teams
