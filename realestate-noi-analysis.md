# Industrial Real Estate NOI & Returns Analysis

This tool calculates key performance metrics for industrial property underwriting and portfolio reviews.

**Primary Metrics Calculated:**
The framework computes NOI (gross rent minus vacancy and operating expenses), cap rate, cash-on-cash return, DSCR, and IRR based on provided financial inputs.

**Key Requirements:**
Users must supply gross potential rent, vacancy assumptions, operating expenses, purchase price, debt terms, equity invested, and hold period details before analysis begins.

**Market Context:**
Current industrial benchmarks suggest cap rates between 5.5–7.5%, with lenders requiring minimum DSCR of 1.20x and target levered IRR ranging from 8–18% depending on strategy (core versus value-add).

**Decision Framework:**
The tool flags three critical risk areas: "DSCR < 1.20x → flag debt coverage risk," vacancy assumptions below 3% in secondary markets indicating optimism bias, and cap rate compression exceeding 100 basis points signaling valuation concerns.

**Output Delivery:**
Results appear in tabular format with metrics, values, and industry benchmarks, followed by 2–3 sentences contextualizing findings for hold/sell decisions or acquisition underwriting.
