# ERP Reconciliation Workflow

This document outlines end-to-end account reconciliation procedures for industrial real estate entities. The workflow covers five primary reconciliation types:

**Bank Reconciliation** ensures GL cash balances match bank statements by tracking outstanding checks, deposits in transit, and bank charges. Any unexplained difference > $100 must be researched.

**Rent Roll to GL Tie-Out** validates that billed rent aligns with executed leases, flagging discrepancies exceeding $500 for investigation.

**AP Sub-Ledger to GL** confirms accounts payable aging totals match GL balances, researching all variances over $1,000.

**Intercompany Reconciliation** verifies that intercompany receivables and payables net to zero across entities.

**Month-End Close Checklist** sequences ten tasks across seven days, from posting cash receipts through financial distribution.

The document requires standardized output including account names, periods, GL balances, reconciled balances, variances, and open items with owners and due dates.

**Critical quality gate:** No reconciliation is complete with an unexplained variance.

This framework ensures audit readiness and maintains internal control standards for multi-property real estate operations.
