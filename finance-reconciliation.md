# Finance: Account Reconciliation

Bank and account reconciliation for industrial real estate entities — GL-to-bank statement matching, sub-ledger verification, and variance investigation.

## Primary Applications
Monthly bank rec for property LLCs and fund entities. Sub-ledger tie-outs for accounts payable, receivable, and fixed assets. Closing procedures and audit preparation.

## Key Workflow Steps
1. Collect GL cash balances, bank statements, prior reconciliations, and known outstanding items
2. Display bank statement balance
3. Adjust for deposits in transit and outstanding checks
4. Compare result to GL balance to identify discrepancies

## Common Variance Sources
- Unrecorded bank fees
- Returned NSF checks
- Timing differences on wire transfers
- Duplicate GL entries
- Miscoded transactions across entities or accounts
- Prior period adjustments

## Documentation Requirements
Reconciliation schedules should present all reconciling items with clear labeling. For unresolved variances, draft explanatory memos identifying dollar amount, root cause, and proposed resolution. Open items list tracks outstanding issues with assigned ownership and target resolution dates.
