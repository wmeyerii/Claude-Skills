# Industrial Real Estate Fund LP Report Generator

This tool creates quarterly investor reports for industrial real estate funds, following a standardized 4–6 page format.

## Key Components

**Required data before drafting:**
- Fund identification (name, quarter, year)
- Fund metrics (NAV, equity deployed, YTD distributions)
- Asset details (occupancy, NOI, lease activity)
- Capital moves (acquisitions, dispositions, refinancings)

**Report sections:**
1. Overview with performance highlights and fund-level metrics table
2. Portfolio breakdown by property (occupancy, NOI, lease activity, capex progress)
3. Capital activity summary
4. Market conditions and supply/demand analysis
5. Distributions and capital account status
6. Forward-looking priorities and risk watch items

## Standards

Be professional but plain-language with specificity in numbers. LPs want numbers first — prefer concrete figures like "$2.1M NOI" over vague qualitative statements. Material negatives must be disclosed transparently rather than minimized. All figures require confirmation before inclusion, and IRR/multiples must align with underlying capital account math.
