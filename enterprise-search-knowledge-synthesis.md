# Enterprise Search: Knowledge Synthesis

Synthesize information from multiple sources into unified summaries for industrial real estate decisions.

## Core Purpose
Combine broker reports, financial data, property documents, and market research into cohesive investment theses and recommendations.

## Four-Step Process
1. **Catalog sources** by type and reliability
2. **Extract facts and claims** from each source
3. **Reconcile disagreements** between sources
4. **Assemble findings** into a unified synthesis

## Output Template
- Headline findings
- Thematic analysis
- Consensus points (where sources agree)
- Documented conflicts (where sources disagree)
- Identified gaps
- Confidence-rated recommendations

## Quality Requirements
- Cite all claims back to source
- Explicitly separate observed facts from derived inferences
- Flag outdated information
- Acknowledge source disagreements transparently — do not obscure conflicts
- Every factual assertion must trace to its source

**Applications:** Due diligence, market analysis, deal evaluation, investment thesis development
