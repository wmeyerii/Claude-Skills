# Marketing: Competitive Brief

Develop market positioning documents for industrial real estate funds and properties.

## Core Purpose
Synthesize competitive positioning, market data, and messaging strategy to support fund marketing, property leasing, and investor communications.

## Key Components

**Market Context**
Vacancy rates, absorption, rental ranges, and pipeline data specific to the geography and asset type.

**Competitive Comparison**
Side-by-side analysis across: size, location, pricing, quality, track record, and differentiation factors.

**Positioning Strategy**
Identify win areas, competitor strengths, and craft audience-specific narratives with proof points.

## Audience Customization

| Audience | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| LPs | Strategy, risk-adjusted returns, team credentials, market selection logic |
| Tenants | Building specs, location advantages, landlord responsiveness, occupancy cost positioning |
| Brokers | Commission structure, space availability, landlord flexibility, co-brokerage track record |

## Deliverable
- Market data summary
- Competitive comparison table
- Clear differentiators
- Messaging frameworks (short / medium / long formats)
- Ready for presentations and marketing collateral
