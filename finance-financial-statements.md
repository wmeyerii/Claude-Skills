# Finance: Financial Statements

Generate and analyze financial statements for industrial real estate funds and property entities. Designed for controllers and fund managers working on monthly reporting cycles.

## Core Statement Types

- **Income Statements** — revenue (base rent, tenant reimbursements, other income) and operating expenses, concluding with NOI and net income
- **Balance Sheets** — assets, liabilities, and equity sections
- **Cash Flow Statements** — direct method, covering operating, investing, and financing activities

## Analysis Protocol

1. Comparative analysis across periods (month-over-month and year-over-year)
2. Budget vs. actual — variance in $ and %
3. Key metrics: NOI margin, expense ratios, debt service coverage
4. Commentary explaining significant variances

## Reporting Standards
GAAP conventions with comparative periods included. Footnotes must address significant transactions, accounting policy changes, and related party transactions. Controller prepares materials for fund manager review before LP or lender distribution.

**Use cases:** LP reporting packages, lender covenant documentation, internal management reporting at asset and fund levels.
