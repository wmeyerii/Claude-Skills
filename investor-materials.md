# Investor Materials

Create cohesive fundraising documents with consistency across all investor-facing assets.

## Core Rule
All investor materials must agree with each other. Before drafting, establish canonical facts for:
- Traction metrics
- Pricing
- Raise size and use of funds
- Team details
- Timelines

## Primary Workflow
1. Catalog your verified facts
2. Define all assumptions
3. Select document type
4. Draft while cross-checking every figure against your established baseline

## Common Assets

**Pitch Decks**
Logical progression: problem → solution → market opportunity → traction → team → funding ask.

**One-Pagers and Memos**
Distill narrative into clear proof points with a precise ask.

**Financial Models**
Explicit assumptions and clean revenue logic. All formulas must reconcile.

**LP Quarterly Updates**
Fund metrics first, then asset-level detail. Numbers before narrative.

## Quality Checklist
Before finalizing any material:
- [ ] Numbers align with source of truth
- [ ] Formulas sum correctly
- [ ] Assumptions are visible, not hidden
- [ ] No unverifiable assertions
- [ ] Market sizing includes methodology
- [ ] Job titles are consistent
- [ ] Revenue calculations reconcile
- [ ] Narrative stands up under scrutiny
