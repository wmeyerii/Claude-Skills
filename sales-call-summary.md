# Sales: Call Summary

Standardized documentation of calls with LPs, tenants, brokers, lenders, and co-investors for CRM records and deal tracking.

## Template
```
CALL SUMMARY
────────────
Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
Attendees: [Names, roles, companies]
Topic: [Brief description]

DISCUSSION POINTS
· [Key point discussed]
· [Key point discussed]

DECISIONS MADE
· [Decision — be specific]

OPEN QUESTIONS
· [Unresolved item]

ACTION ITEMS
| Action | Owner | Due Date | Priority |
|--------|-------|----------|----------|
| [Task] | [Name] | [Date] | [H/M/L] |

NEXT STEPS / FOLLOW-UP
[Scheduled next contact, date, purpose]
```

## CRM Entry Standard
2–3 line summary with next steps. Example: "Discussed Q2 distribution timing. LP confirmed $5M–$10M allocation interest for Fund III. Follow up with deck by [date]."

## Best Practices
- Record commitments verbatim, not inferences
- Include specific numbers ("$5M–$10M allocation" not "interested in investing")
- Flag relationship sentiment shifts
- Distinguish resolved decisions from open questions
- Assign clear ownership and due dates for every action item

**Users:** Asset Managers and Fund Managers | **Frequency:** Daily
